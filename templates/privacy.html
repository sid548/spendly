Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, actions taken).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Service:

  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating Spendly.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load the typefaces used in the interface. Google may log font requests per their own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

5. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. To request deletion, contact us using the details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app.
